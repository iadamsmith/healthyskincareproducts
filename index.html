Understanding Pakistan's Climate and Its Impact on Skin

Pakistan experiences long sunny days for most of the year. Excessive UV exposure can lead to:

2. Humidity and Pollution

  • Clog pores

3. Seasonal Dryness

During winter months, dry air can lead to:

  • Reduced skin glow

Skincare products used in Pakistan must therefore balance brightening, hydration, and protection.
